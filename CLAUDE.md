# CLAUDE.md — onepercentbetter.poker

## What this is

Poker-specific front door for the 1% Better product line.

- Domain target: `onepercentbetter.poker`
- Role: position the poker tools as serious analytics and workflow products
- Relationship to `.dev`: same maker, same brand system, different audience and tone

## Brand Guardrails

- Keep it minimal, but not soft
- Use poker language when it improves clarity
- Position around analysis, decision support, review, and edge
- Do not make this read like a general personal portfolio
- Do not let the site become a bigger project than the products it introduces

## Product Story

Current product family:

- `1% Better - Exploit Better`
- `1% Better - Action Keeper`

The site can mention future tools, but should keep the current story tight.

## Visual Direction

- same design family as `.dev`
- darker palette
- sharper contrast
- cleaner stat/instrument feel
- less paper, more table/terminal/analysis board
